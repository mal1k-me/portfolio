Professional Events

Activities & Achievements

From IEEE activities to professional development, exploring various opportunities in technology and engineering.

IIT Job Fair Exploration

Taking proactive steps towards professional development by attending job fairs organized at IIT. This initiative is part of our curriculum's focus on industry exposure, helping us understand potential internship opportunities and professional requirements in the software industry.

IEEE CS: Flutter Weather App Workshop

Participated in an engaging workshop organized by IEEE Computer Society Chapter where we built a weather application using Flutter and REST APIs. This hands-on experience introduced me to mobile development, API integration, and modern UI design patterns.

NVIDIA Deep Learning Certification

Participated in a collaborative program between IEEE Student Branches of IIT and ENIS, completing NVIDIA's Fundamentals of Deep Learning certification. This experience provided valuable insights into AI and machine learning fundamentals.

IEEE Xtreme Programming Competition

Embraced the challenge of competitive programming through IEEE Xtreme at IIT. This 24-hour global competition was an exciting introduction to the world of algorithmic problem-solving and coding under pressure!

IEEE RAS Chapter Activities

Active member of the IEEE Student Branch Robotics and Automation Society Chapter. Started with introductory robotics using Arduino and continuing to expand knowledge in automation and robotics through ongoing chapter activities and workshops.
